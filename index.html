Apostolos Kalatzis

I am Apostolos Kalatzis, a Ph.D. student (joined Fall-2019) in Computer Science at Montana State University, Bozeman where I study Human-Robot Interaction. My advisor is Dr. Laura Stanley. My research focuses on human-artificial intelligence collaboration and the mechanisms to monitor the cognitive state of the human working next to a robot. I am currently working in a project to develop a digital interface with a layer of augmented reality to improve worker safety and satisfaction. I earned a B.Sc. in Computer Science from University of West Attica, Greece in 2013 and a M.Sc. in Computer Science from California State University, Los Angeles in 2019.
